## What is VCloud?

VCloud, your very own Virtual Cloud, is a haven for those who seek to replicate the power and flexibility of a cloud environment. This homelab is engineered to operate on your premises while maintaining the potential to seamlessly transition into a public cloud environment. VCloud thrives on versatility, adaptability, and, most importantly, your curiosity.

### Key Features
- **Privacy Focus**: Your privacy matters. VCloud places a strong emphasis on keeping your data and activities private, ensuring that your homelab experience remains secure and discreet.
- **Unraid OS at the Core**: The heart of VCloud beats with the robust Unraid operating system, providing stability, flexibility, and efficient resource management. It's the bedrock of your homelab exploration.
- **Dockerized Awesomeness**: Embrace the power of Docker containers within VCloud. These lightweight, portable containers encapsulate services and applications, promoting seamless operation and simplified management.
